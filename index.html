Who Are We

Our Story

JUDES Community Based Organization was founded and registered in 2018 in Homabay County, inspired by St. Jude, the patron of the desperate. It was established to address critical gaps in underserved rural communities, where health, education, and social equity challenges were deeply interconnected.

Our beginnings were rooted in collaboration between health professionals, disability advocates, and community leaders determined to break systemic barriers such as stigma, lack of accessible services, and cultural taboos. Since then, we have grown into a trusted force for grassroots transformation and advocacy.

Our Mission

To prioritize the needs of the vulnerable and less fortunate by addressing interconnected challenges in education, health, economic empowerment, and social justice. Our mission is to bridge gaps that limit opportunity, eliminate barriers to access, and nurture environments where dignity, respect, and self-sufficiency are at the core of development.

We achieve this by engaging with local communities, designing sustainable programs, and promoting collective action that strengthens resilience and drives lasting social transformation.

Our Vision

A compassionate and inclusive community where everyone has equal access to resources, opportunities, and support systems needed to thrive. We envision a society where poverty, stigma, and inequality no longer hinder progress, and individuals are empowered to live with dignity, purpose, and fulfillment.

By fostering solidarity, innovation, and accountability, we aim to inspire future generations and build a culture that leaves no one behind.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We care deeply about the well-being of our community members, offering support and kindness especially in times of vulnerability.

Empowerment

We believe in the potential of individuals to create positive change. Through skills, knowledge, and resources, we enable people to take charge of their own development.

Inclusivity

We celebrate diversity and ensure equal opportunities for all, regardless of gender, ability, or background. Inclusivity drives the strength and unity of our community.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and ethics in every program and decision, building trust within the communities we serve.

Community

We believe in collaboration, partnerships, and collective action. By working together, we achieve greater impact and sustainable solutions.

Our Programs

We are committed to transforming lives through sustainable, inclusive, and impactful programs designed to meet the unique needs of our community.

Agricultural Development

We empower local farmers with modern, sustainable farming practices that improve yields while protecting the environment. Our program focuses on climate-smart agriculture, access to quality seeds, training on organic farming, and post-harvest handling. By encouraging agribusiness ventures, we help families achieve food security and generate income, reducing poverty and hunger in the community.

Education Support

We believe education is the foundation for a brighter future. Through this program, we provide scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship opportunities for vulnerable children and youth. We also run after-school learning initiatives, digital literacy training, and capacity-building for teachers. Our goal is to break barriers to education and empower learners to achieve their full potential.

Women Empowerment

This program focuses on enhancing the socio-economic independence of women and girls. We provide vocational training, entrepreneurship skills, and access to microfinance opportunities that enable women to start and sustain small businesses. Additionally, we raise awareness on gender equality, reproductive health, and leadership, ensuring women become active decision-makers in their homes and communities.

Youth Development

We nurture young people to be agents of positive change by offering mentorship, skills training, and leadership development programs. Our focus is on equipping youth with entrepreneurial skills, digital knowledge, and opportunities to participate in community development initiatives. We also run campaigns on drug abuse prevention, mental health, and civic engagement to ensure holistic growth.

Health & Wellness

Access to quality healthcare is vital for a thriving community. We organize health camps, awareness campaigns, and partnerships with medical professionals to provide free checkups, maternal health support, and preventive care. Our focus includes nutrition education, HIV/AIDS awareness, and mental health support to promote overall community well-being.
